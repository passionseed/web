# PassionSeed - Executive Summary

## Company Overview

**PassionSeed** is an AI-powered career discovery platform helping Southeast Asian high school students "test-drive" career paths before committing years to the wrong direction.

---

## The Problem

Students in SEA choose academic majors and career paths without real-world testing, leading to:
- **Wasted time**: 3-4 years on wrong degrees
- **Financial loss**: $10,000+ in tuition per wrong choice
- **Career regret**: 70% of graduates work in unrelated fields
- **Mental health**: Anxiety, imposter syndrome, lack of direction

---

## The Solution

**Expert Interview AI Bot** - Our proprietary AI converts professional knowledge into interactive career quests:

1. **Expert Input**: Professionals share domain knowledge via structured surveys
2. **AI Processing**: Our AI transforms responses into interactive learning modules
3. **Student Testing**: Students "test-drive" careers through hands-on simulations
4. **Data Insights**: Personalized recommendations based on student interactions
