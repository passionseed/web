   - Currently part-time (students)

   - No salary until funding

2. **Early Employees**:
   - Start as contractors (flexible, lower commitment)
   - Convert to full-time employees after seed funding
   - Use standard Thai employment contracts

3. **Interns**:
   - Use university internship programs
   - 3-6 month commitments
   - Academic credit + stipend

**Thai Employment Law Considerations**:
- Probationary period: Max 120 days
- Severance: Required after 120 days
- Social security: 5% employer + 5% employee
- Work permits: Not needed for Thai nationals

## Step 13: Value Proposition Learning (2 Hours)

**Key Points Learned**:

1. **Value Proposition = Clear Benefit + Differentiation**
   - Not just "what you do" but "why it matters"
   - Must be specific, not generic

2. **Marketing Channels Must Match Product**
   - B2C EdTech: TikTok, student communities, schools
   - Don't chase channels that don't fit

3. **Product-Channel Fit is Critical**
   - Our product: Mobile-first, gamified, short sessions
   - Best channels: TikTok (viral), Discord (community), LINE (parents)

4. **Primary Positioning Benefit**
   - "Test-drive your career in 15 minutes"
   - Clear, specific, actionable
