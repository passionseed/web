## The Pitch

Hi, I'm Bunyasit, founder of PassionSeed.

Every year, millions of Southeast Asian students pour time and money into building portfolios—projects, hackathons, competitions—hoping it'll get them into their dream university or job. But they have no idea if what they're building actually matters.

**We tell them.**

PassionSeed is a career discovery platform built on Ikigai and future adaptability. Here's how it works:

**First, we bring students in through events and hackathons** where they build real portfolios—not just busy work.

**Then, our Portfolio Grading Engine scores their work** against what top universities and employers actually accept. A student uploads their hackathon project and learns: "Your portfolio has a 73% acceptance rate for computer science at Chula, but only 34% for data science. Here's what to add to improve."

**For students who aren't sure what path to pursue, PathLab** helps them discover careers that match their Ikigai—what they love, what they're good at, what the world needs, and what can pay them—plus how future-proof that career is.

**Traction:** 349 hackathon sign-ups, 1,000+ Discord members, 66% team formation rate.

**We're raising $500K to $1M** to scale across Southeast Asia's $400M high school EdTech market.

**Build your portfolio. Know your odds. Find your path.** That's PassionSeed.
