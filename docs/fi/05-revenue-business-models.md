## Step 3: Revenue Models

**Task**: Identify two potential revenue models with pros/cons and pricing strategy.

**Submitted Answer**:

### Model 1: Metered Usage
Student only pays on what they use, research, AI features

**Pros**:
- profitable at any scale
- easy to get started
- transparent to every user

**Cons**:
- easy to churn
- user optimized for least usage
- less predictable revenue

### Model 2: Employer Talent Access
Students use the platform free; employers pay to access talent pools or portfolios.

**Pros**:
- Students remain free users = easier growth
- Employers have budgets; revenue per customer is higher
- Clear value: better talent discovery
- Network effects if student profiles become valuable

**Cons**:
- Requires large student supply before employers care
- Employers expect filtering, verification, ranking quality
- Students may feel exploited if data is sold without clear benefit
