## Deliverables Summary

### Step 1: Alternatives
- **Alternative 1: B2B to Schools**
  - Benefits: Recurring revenue, direct access to large student groups, schools become distribution partners
  - Negatives: Long sales cycles (3-6 months), limited school budgets, requires curriculum alignment
- **Alternative 2: B2C2C to Parents**
  - Benefits: Higher willingness to pay, word-of-mouth among parents, parents control spending
  - Negatives: Higher CAC to reach parents, parents not the end user, complex decision-making unit
- **Final Decision**: Stay with hackathon-to-app conversion. Hackathon students are already engaged. Converting them to app users is the lowest friction path to product-market fit.
