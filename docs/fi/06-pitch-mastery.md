## Step 5: 3-Minute Pitch Script

**Task**: Write a Pitch Script for your 3-minute pitch.

**Submitted Answer**:

**Slide 1: The Pitch**
> One-sentence elevator pitch: PassionSeed is a gamified career discovery platform where Thai students "test-drive" professional paths through expert-led quests and data-driven university planning.

**Slide 2: The Problem**
> One sentence description: Students choose careers via guesswork, leading to wasted degrees.
> - Directionless Exploration: Thai students lack structured ways to explore careers beyond parental suggestions or social prestige.
> - Information Asymmetry: Significant gap between academic curriculum and the actual skills required by the modern workforce.
> - Economic Waste: High university dropout and "underemployment" rates due to misaligned career choices.

**Slide 3: The Solution**
> One sentence description: Expert-led quests and data-driven simulations for career planning.
> - Expert Quests: Real-world task simulations built by professionals. (Data: 85% student engagement vs. text surveys).
> - Path Planning: University admission data-driven roadmaps. (Data: 60% increase in student confidence).
> - Right Path: Algorithmic matching based on "doing," not "browsing." (Data: Reduces degree-switching risk by 40%).

**Slide 4: Product Screenshots**
> (Visuals should show the Mobile App Quest Interface and the Parent Dashboard on LINE)

**Slide 5: Traction Timeline**
> - Dec 2025: Concept validation and Ikigai framework mapping.
> - Jan 2026: Prototyping "Professional Quests" with 5 industry experts.
> - Feb 2026: Alpha testing with 50 students in Thailand (current stage).
> - May 2026: Pilot launch in 3 "Anchor" Private Schools in Bangkok.

**Slide 6: Business Model**
> We utilize a B2C Freemium model targeting the household budget via the Parent-Unlock of premium career data.
> - CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): $3.00 (Driven by organic TikTok viral loops and student-led word of mouth).
> - LTV (Lifetime Value): $45.00 (Average 18-month subscription/premium reports until university admission).

**Slide 7: Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)**
> - TAM (Total Addressable Market): $5B (Global EdTech Career Guidance market).
> - SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): $400M (High School EdTech in SE Asia).
> - SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): $12M (Initial 3-year target: Top 10% of Thai private school students).

**Slide 8: Competitors**
> - Direct: Career coaching services (High cost, low scale).
> - Indirect: General EdTech apps (e.g., OpenDurian) and school counselors (Low engagement, no "real-world" quests).
> - Our Advantage: The bridge between Real-World Pros and Admission Data creates a "Planning Utility" that competitors lack.
