# The Next Decade Hackathon - Context & Overview

## 🎯 Core Mission
We empower a new generation of innovators to shape healthcare that is preventive, predictive, and grounded in evidence and empathy.

## 🌍 The Environment & Approach
We create an inclusive, hands-on environment where people from any background can learn to explore health challenges, work with research, and build preventive and predictive solutions together.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **High School Students**
- **University Students**

## 🚀 Key Goals & Outcomes
- **AI Readiness:** We will train participants to be AI-ready, equipping them with the skills and tools needed to tackle modern healthcare challenges.
- **Method Validation:** The hackathon serves as a proving ground to validate and prove our educational and collaborative methods in real-time.
