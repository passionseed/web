## Choice of MVP & Rationale

### Selected MVP: Hackathon Registration + Beta Waitlist

**Rationale:**
The hackathon registration served as the primary conversion goal, driving 349 sign-ups with a strong 66.2% team formation rate. This indicates high engagement and commitment from participants.

**Beta Waitlist Integration:**
The beta waitlist captured 29 highly interested users who opted in for early access, representing a qualified lead pool for product launch.

## Customer Segments Discovered

### Segment 1: Competition-Oriented Students
- **Needs:** Competition experience, portfolio building, prizes
- **Pain Points:** Limited hackathon opportunities, difficulty forming teams
- **Insight:** High engagement when team formation is facilitated

### Segment 2: Career-Focused Developers
- **Needs:** Networking, skill development, career opportunities
- **Pain Points:** Limited exposure to real-world projects
- **Insight:** Attracted by practical, hands-on experience

### Segment 3: Early Adopters
- **Needs:** Early access to new tools, exclusive features
- **Pain Points:** FOMO, desire to be first
- **Insight:** Beta waitlist captured this segment effectively

## Results Evaluation

### Successes
1. **Exceeded registration target by 3.5x** (349 vs 100 target)
2. **High team formation rate** (66.2%) indicates engaged participants
3. **Strong Instagram performance** validated platform choice
4. **Email open rate above industry average** (20.39% vs 18% avg)

### Areas for Improvement
1. **Beta conversion rate** could be higher (29/349 = 8.3%)
2. **Email CTR** slightly below target (1.94% vs 2%+)
3. **Cross-channel attribution** could be improved

## Key Learnings

### What Worked
- Instagram as primary channel proved highly effective
- Team formation feature increased engagement
- Clear value proposition attracted target audience
- Email campaign had solid open rates

### What to Fix
- Increase beta sign-up conversion rate
- Improve email call-to-action for higher CTR
- Better integration between channels
