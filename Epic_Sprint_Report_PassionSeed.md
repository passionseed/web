## Email Campaign: Lessons Learned

### Campaign Execution
- **Recipients:** 208 (existing mailing list)
- **Batches Sent:** 1
- **Platform:** Resend
- **Deliverability Issue:** Routed to Gmail Promotions tab

### Performance Analysis

| Metric | Value | Industry Avg | Assessment |
|--------|-------|--------------|------------|
| Delivered to Inbox | ~20% | 85%+ | ❌ Critical issue |
| Open Rate (of delivered) | ~20% | 18-22% | ✅ Good content |
| CTR (of opened) | ~10% | 2-3% | ✅ Strong CTAs |
| Conversions | ~15 | — | ⚠️ Low volume |

### Root Cause Analysis
**Why Email Failed to Scale:**
1. **Domain reputation:** New sending domain (passionseed.org) not warmed up
2. **Content filters:** "Hackathon," "prizes," "register" triggered promotions
3. **Single batch:** No opportunity to iterate on deliverability
4. **Gmail dominance:** Most student emails are Gmail (affected heavily)

### Recovery Plan
**Immediate (Week 1):**
- Set up dedicated sending domain with warming sequence
- Remove promotional trigger words from subject lines
- Segment engaged users from Instagram to seed reputation

**Short-term (Month 1):**
- A/B test plain-text vs HTML emails
- Implement DKIM/SPF authentication
- Gradual volume ramp: 50 → 100 → 200 → full list

**Long-term (Ongoing):**
- Sunset unengaged subscribers monthly
- Maintain <0.1% complaint rate
- Use email for retention, not acquisition (until fixed)
